Description

Enter the Salty Seabird Race!
Play sweet mini-games with your mateys and see who's crowned the pirate king and who ends up being the salty seabird.
Salty Seabird Bay is the ultimate mix of pirate themed minigames.
Compete in treasure hunting, play scallywag cards - the pirate card game, and engage in sea battles.
But watch out for Krakens!

Features

  • Three Minigames And more to be announced.
  • Local Multiplayer Bonanza 2 to 4 players on the same screen.
  • Pirates! Based on the golden age of piracy.
  • Grand Race mode See who's the king of all pirate games.
  • In-game Avatars Pick your favourite out of many crazy swashbucklers.
  • Colourful and Fun Retro Style Pirates in 16-bit style glory. Nostalgic pixel art and soundtrack.
